Right to Freedom of Religion

Article 25 Article 26 Article 27 Article 28

Article 25

Freedom of Conscience and Religion

  • Everyone has the right to freely practice, profess, and propagate their religion, subject to public order and health.

Article 26

Freedom to Manage Religious Affairs

  • Religious groups can manage their institutions, own property, and administer affairs.

Article 27

No Tax for Promoting a Religion

  • No one is required to pay taxes for the promotion of a particular religion.

Article 28

Freedom from Religious Instruction

  • Religious instruction is prohibited in state-funded educational institutions.
