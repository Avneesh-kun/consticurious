Right to Constitutional Remedies

Article 32 Article 33 Article 34 Article 35

Article 32

Right to Approach the Supreme Court

  • Citizens can directly approach the Supreme Court if their Fundamental Rights are violated.
  • The court can issue five types of writs to enforce rights: Habeas Corpus, Mandamus, Prohibition, Quo Warranto, and Certiorari.

Article 33

Restriction on Fundamental Rights for Armed Forces

  • Parliament can restrict Fundamental Rights for armed forces to ensure discipline and security.

Article 34

Suspension of Rights During Martial Law

  • Fundamental Rights may be restricted in areas where martial law is declared.

Article 35

Power to Make Laws to Enforce Rights

  • Parliament can make laws to enforce certain Fundamental Rights.
