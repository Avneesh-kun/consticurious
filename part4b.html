Article 38

Promotion of Welfare

  • The State shall strive to create a social order that ensures justice (social, economic, and political) for all.
  • Reduce inequalities in income, status, and opportunities.

Article 39

Certain Principles of Policy

  • The State shall ensure:
    1. Adequate means of livelihood for all citizens.
    2. Equal pay for equal work for men and women.
    3. Protection of children from exploitation and ensuring their development.
    4. Fair distribution of wealth and resources to avoid concentration in a few hands.

Article 39A

Equal Justice and Legal Aid

  • The State shall provide free legal aid to ensure equal access to justice for all citizens.

Article 40

Organization of Village Panchayats

  • The State shall promote village self-governance by organizing panchayats.

Article 41

Right to Work, Education, and Public Assistance

  • The State shall provide the right to work, education, and assistance for the unemployed, sick, and elderly, depending on its resources.

Article 42

Just and Humane Conditions of Work and Maternity Relief

  • The State shall ensure safe and humane working conditions and provide maternity relief.

Article 43

Living Wage for Workers

  • Workers shall receive a fair living wage, decent working conditions, and access to social and cultural opportunities.
