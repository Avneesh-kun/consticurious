Article 49

Protection of Monuments and National Heritage

  • The State shall protect historic monuments and places of artistic and cultural importance.

Article 50

Separation of Judiciary from Executive

  • The State shall ensure the independence of the judiciary from the executive.

Article 51

Promotion of International Peace

  • The State shall:
    1. Foster international peace and security.
    2. Respect international law and treaties.
    3. Promote settlement of disputes through peaceful means.
