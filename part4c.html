Article 44

Uniform Civil Code

  • The State shall strive to implement a Uniform Civil Code (UCC) across the country to ensure uniform laws for all citizens, regardless of religion.

Article 45

Early Childhood Care and Education

  • The State shall provide free early childhood care and education for children under the age of six years.

Article 46

Promotion of Educational and Economic Interests of Weaker Sections

  • The State shall promote the interests of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and other weaker sections to protect them from exploitation.

Article 47

Duty of the State to Raise Nutrition and Health Standards

  • The State shall improve public health, nutrition, and living standards, and prohibit intoxicating substances except for medicinal purposes.

Article 48

Organization of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry

  • The State shall modernize agriculture and prohibit the slaughter of cows and calves to protect the environment and rural economy.

Article 48A

Protection and Improvement of Environment

  • The State shall protect forests, wildlife, and the environment.
