Article 12

Definition of "State"

  • The term "State" includes the government and its instrumentalities (e.g., Parliament, state legislatures, local authorities).
    Fundamental Rights are enforceable against the State.

Article 2

Laws Inconsistent with Fundamental Rights

  • Any law that violates Fundamental Rights is void.
  • Parliament cannot make laws that infringe upon Fundamental Rights.
